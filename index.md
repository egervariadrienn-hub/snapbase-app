## User Manual

### 1. Getting Started
When you first open SnapBase, tap **"Start Scanning"**. The app will request permission to access your media and silently index your latest screenshots (up to 42 for free users).

### 2. Searching
Use the search bar at the top to type any keyword. SnapBase will filter your gallery based on the text found within the screenshots, the tags you've added, or the filenames.

### 3. Organizing with Stacks
To group multiple images (e.g., several pages of an article):
1. Tap the **Paperclip icon** to enter Selection Mode.
2. Select the images you want to group.
3. Tap **"Merge"**. They will now appear as a single stack with a count indicator.

### 4. Viewing & Extracting Text
Tap any image to view it in full screen.
- **Add Tag**: Tap the "+ Tag" button to add a custom category.
- **Copy Text**: Expand the **"Detected text"** section and tap **"Copy to Clipboard"** to grab the text.
- **Delete**: Enter Delete Mode via the Trash icon to remove unwanted items from the app database.
