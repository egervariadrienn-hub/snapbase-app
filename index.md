# Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All image processing, text recognition (OCR), tagging, and filtering
are performed entirely on the user's device.

The app accesses photos and media files stored on the device
only to provide its core functionality.

No data is sent to external servers.
No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
